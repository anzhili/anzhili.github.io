Welcome to my webpage! I am a Master's student in Statistics and Applied Economics at the University of Georgia. Also, I work as a statistical consultant at the Statistical Consulting Center and research assistant at the Department of Agricultural and Applied Economics at the University of Georgia. My research focuses on quantitative and qualitative forecasting of agricultural commodity prices using Bayesian analysis.
